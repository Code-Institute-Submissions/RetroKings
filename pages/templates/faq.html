CUSTOMER CARE UPDATE

Our Customer Care Team are busier than usual during these uncertain times, but we’re listening to what you need.
We’re still here to help you, but to get the answers to our most popular asked questions please see below.

You can create an account by clicking on the "My Account" link at the top of the page, or simply click here.

Fill in your returns slip and put it in your parcel. We’ll need to know the item you’re returning and the reason you’re returning it to us.

  • Returns are FREE and trackable.
  • You have 28 days from the date you receive your order to return it for a refund.
  • Returning your order within 29 to 45 days? A gift voucher will be issued instead.
  • It can take up to 14 working days for your order to reach the warehouse and be processed.
  • Refunds can take up to 10 working days to show on your account.
All returned items should be sent in their original condition and packaging including tags etc. In the unlikely event that an item is returned to us in an unsuitable condition, we may send it back to you.

If your order has been sent to you using a trackable service, you can follow its journey to you. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email from our warehouse once your order is on its way; simply click on your tracking link on the email to view the up to date tracking.

All orders dispatch within 24 hours, but it may take up to 3 business days depending on different circumstances. While we would like to ensure your order is delivered swiftly, we use companies such as (An Post, DHL & UPS) so we cannot guarantee delivery times, or be held responsible for conditions beyond our control.

The Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013 (IRE) advise you have fourteen calendar days to cancel the contract for your order with us and we will issue you with a full refund for the items you have purchased and the cost of standard delivery if you've paid for shipping.
